Answers to FAQs

What were INXS originally called?
Top
INXS were originally calledThe Farriss Brothers. They were founded in 1977 in Sydney, New South Wales. There were three Farriss brothers in the band: Andrew Farriss, Jon Farriss, and Tim Farriss. Andrew Farriss was the main composer and keyboardist, Jon Farriss was the drummer, and Tim Farriss was the guitarist
Who was INXS's lead singer?
Top
Michael Hutchence. He was born January 22, 1960, and died in November 22 1997. He co-founded INXS. INXS has sold over 50 million records worldwide and were inducted to the Australian Recording Industry Association in 2001. He was the lead singer and lyricst for INXS. In July 1996, he and Paula Yates had a daughter. He was also an actor.
How did the lead singer die?
Top
On November 22, 1997, Michael Hutchence was found dead in his hotel room. After being involved with drugs and alcohol, he committed suicide by hanging himself. There was also an on going custody battle between him and Paula Yates with their daughter. Michael Hutchence was frightened and was troubled by the custody battle. The death was ruled suicide while depressed and under the influence of alcohol and other drugs.
What years were INXS active?
Top
INXS started in 1977 with the Farriss brothers, Andrew Farriss, Jon Farriss, and Tim Farriss. In 1978, three more members joined, Michael Hutchence, Garry Gary Beers, and Kirk Pengilly. After Michael Hutchence died, INXS did not perform publiclyy for almost a year. They made a few performances with different guest singers untill the year 2000. Around 2004, the band hosted contests to find a new lead singer for the band. INXS toured in Australia and New Zealand in 2007. The band continued to tour, doing performances for the Olympic Winter Games and other performances up until 2012.
What was INXS's most popular song?
Top
The song Need You Tonight is INXS's most popular song. It was released in 1987 as part of their Kick album. This was the only INXS song that reached number one on the Billboard Hot 100. Other songs, such as What You Need was a top five Billboard hit. All their songs were good though, I would say they were all well rounded, which is what you want if you are writing music. There is no point in having one hit song, if we cannot replicate the experience and put some of that energy into all the songs. Even though Need You Tonight is the most popular song according to charts, my favorite song from INXS was Mystify. It shows that INXS music applies to lots of different tastes. I would also say Never Tear Us Apart is another one of my favorites that is not a major hit on the charts.
Is INXS still around?
Top
After 35 years, the band played their last performance in Perth, Australia in 2012. They did not say the band would permanently retire though. In an Australian television interview, Jon Farriss said Never say never, meaning the band might record and play more music in the future. The years after 2012 included a television miniseries about the band. And in 2019, a documentary film called Mystify was released about Michael Hutchence.
What type of music does INXS play?
Top
INXS played what is called new wave/pop style music. Later, the band developed a pub style rock that included funk and dance elements. I would say they sound similar to the Fine Young Cannibals in their hit She Drives Me Crazy. It is like it is rock, but it is not like Led Zeppelin, or The Rolling Stones. Music from the previous two artists is kind of hard. INXS was more upbeat and had a touch of uplifting to it. I would also use the term pop to describe their music because it is more like U2, or Coldplay. Not really hard, but softer and well rounded.
Who was the latest lead singer for INXS?
Top
The latest lead singer was Ciaran Gribbin. He is a Northern Irish singer-songwriter, guitarist and producer. J.D. Fortune was the previous lead singer for INXS. He was the winner of the competition INXS hosted to search for a new lead singer. The band also recorded an album in memory of Michael Hutchence called Original Sin. Ben Harper, Patrick Monahan from the band Train, and Rob Thomas from the band Matchbox Twenty were all featured in the album. Train, and Matchbox Twenty and both of their lead singers are another favorite of mine too. Patrick Monahan has some real talent as seen in his Drops of Jupiter song. And Rob Thomas has several good hits as well.
What was INXS's best selling album?
Top
Kick was INXS's best selling album. It was certified six times platinum and it peaked at number three on the Billboard 200. Sales for Kick were about 20 million by 2012. Rolling Stone rated Kick at number three in Best Albums of the Year in 1988. Other albums are INXS, Underneath the Colours, X, and Elegantly Wasted.
How did INXS get started?
Top
Andrew Farriss had a band called Doctor Dolphin and he asked his then high school classmate, Michael Hutchence to join him. The band had a few other people. After that the Farriss brothers teamed up with Garry Gary Beers and Kirk Pengilly and made their debut on August 16, 1977 at Whale Beach, north of Sydney. The band's first performance as INXS was on September 1, 1979, at the Ocean Beach Hotel in Umina, New South Wales.
Did Michael Hutchence have a favorite album or albums?
Top
Yes, he liked Kick,Welcome to Wherever You Are, and Elegantly Wasted.
Was Michael Hutchence friends with any other famous people or musicians?
Top
Yes, he was friends with the lead singer from the Irish band U2, Bono, and the band Duran Duran. Bono broke off their friendship though three years before Michael Hutchence's suicide because of his drug use.
